About

Hey there! I'm Karim, and I absolutely love bringing ideas to life through design and code. My journey into this creative world started when I was just messing around with Photoshop and basic HTML during high school - and I've been hooked ever since! What really gets me excited is the whole process of creating something from scratch - whether it's designing a slick user interface, crafting a brand's visual identity, or coding up a responsive website. There's something magical about taking an idea from concept to reality, you know? I'm entirely self-taught, and honestly, that's what makes this journey so special to me. Every new skill I've picked up has come from genuine curiosity and countless hours of trial and error. I remember staying up late watching tutorials, breaking (and fixing) code, and redesigning the same projects multiple times until they felt just right. Over the past 3 years, I've developed a pretty solid toolkit. I'm fluent in the Adobe Creative Suite, absolutely love working in Figma, and can hand-code responsive websites that look great on any device. But what really drives me is the problem-solving aspect - figuring out how to make designs not just beautiful, but truly functional and user-friendly. Right now, I'm focused on building up my portfolio with projects that showcase both my design and development skills. I'm also diving deeper into UI/UX principles and exploring the world of interactive web animations. When I'm not designing or coding, you'll probably find me analyzing other websites and apps, trying to figure out what makes them tick. I'm always open to collaborations and new opportunities, especially ones that challenge me to grow and learn something new.
