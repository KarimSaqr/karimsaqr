About

I’m a passionate self-taught designer and front-end developer with a strong focus on graphic design, UI/UX, and building clean, user-friendly web experiences. My journey began with pure curiosity — exploring tools like Figma, coding websites from scratch, and studying the design of everyday products, apps, and interfaces around me. Over time, this curiosity turned into a deep commitment to creative problem-solving through design. I specialize in creating visually compelling, functional designs that not only look good but also serve a purpose. Whether it's crafting a brand identity, designing intuitive UI layouts, or coding a responsive website, I aim to bring clarity, simplicity, and intention to everything I build. With over 3 years of hands-on practice, I’ve built a solid foundation in modern design tools and web technologies — continuously learning, experimenting, and refining my skills to stay sharp and adaptable in a fast-moving digital world. I’m currently working on building my portfolio, exploring personal projects, and preparing to take my skills into the freelance and professional space.
